``polaroid``
============

*Hyper Fast and safe image manipulation for python. Powered by rust.*


About
-----

``polaroid`` is a fast and safe image manipulation for python. Powered by rust
Binds a variety of libraries like imagers, photon_rs and image proc with a nice pythonic interface.
